Our Firm

About Us

Thorrington-Smith and Silver was established by its present partners, David Thorrington-Smith and Wade Silver in 1995. We are a boutique law firm with offices in La Lucia specializing in personal injury law, in particular claims against the Road Accident Fund.

With over 25 years of experience, we have built a proven track record of excellent results. Thorrington-Smith & Silver strives to offer a professional, personal, and efficient service to all our valued clients.

Our Services

What We Do?

We advise and assist you in gathering information and documentation, engage with medical experts to quantify your claim, and appoint skilled advocates in the event of your claim being litigated.

RAF Claims

Assist you in claiming the maximum compensation you are entitled to from the Road Accident Fund when you have sustained serious bodily injuries in a motor-vehicle accident which was not your fault.

Public Liability Claims

If you have sustained injuries from a variety of causes and you were not at fault, we will assist you in claiming compensation from the wrongdoer.

Medical Malpractice Claims

If you have suffered damages as a result of medical negligence we will assisst you in claiming against the wrongdoer.
